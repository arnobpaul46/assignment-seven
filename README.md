# 🌿 KeenKeeper

**KeenKeeper** is your personal shelf of meaningful connections. In a fast-paced world, it’s easy to lose touch with the people who matter most. KeenKeeper helps you browse, tend, and nurture your relationships by tracking your interactions and reminding you when it's time to reach out.

---

## 🚀 Technologies Used

This project was built using a modern frontend stack for a fast and responsive experience:

- **Frontend Library:** [React.js](https://reactjs.org/) (Vite)
- **Styling:** [Tailwind CSS](https://tailwindcss.com/) & [DaisyUI](https://daisyui.com/)
- **Routing:** [React Router Dom v7](https://reactrouter.com/)
- **Icons:** [Lucide-React](https://lucide-react.dev/)
- **Charts/Analytics:** [Recharts](https://recharts.org/)
- **Notifications:** [React Toastify](https://fkhadra.github.io/react-toastify/)
- **Loading States:** [React Spinners](https://www.davidhu.io/react-spinners/) (PacmanLoader)

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 1. Dynamic Relationship Tracking
Manage a personal catalog of friends and family with real-time status indicators. The app categorizes your connections into **On-Track**, **Almost Due**, or **Overdue** based on your last contact, ensuring no important relationship is neglected.

### 2. Interactive Interaction Timeline
Never forget the last time you spoke. Every time you log a call, text, or video chat, it gets added to a beautiful, dashed-border timeline. With built-in filtering, you can easily sort your history to see exactly how you've been staying in touch.

### 3. Friendship Analytics Dashboard
Gain insights into your social habits through visual data. KeenKeeper uses interactive **Pie Charts** to show the distribution of your interaction types (Calls vs. Texts vs. Videos), helping you understand and balance your communication style.
